Green Arch
★ ⊹₊ ⋆ Editorial design
Sustainability is a term we use and hear about almost every day. The importance of maintaining and improving the environment through sustainable practices is crucial for our future, as are other forms of sustainability that we should consider. The practice of art and design has an important role to play in promoting sustainability, particularly through raising awareness. As a specialized field within the creative industries, design should promote debate and interaction, working towards possible ways of promoting sustainable social structures. I was invited, in an academic setting, to develop an editorial project about sustainability.
