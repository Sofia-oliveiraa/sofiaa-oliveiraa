Grey Fuse
❤︎₊ ⊹ Collaborative project
At COP26 in 2021, governments worldwide committed to achieving net-zero carbon emissions. This goal requires a considerable transformation in industries, infrastructure, and behaviors. On the other hand, the most recent COP27 in 2022, established a fund to assist vulnerable nations in dealing with climate-related disasters but fell short in deepening the urgent discussion on reducing global greenhouse gas emissions. The theme "Art, Design, and Media in response to the Climate Crisis" challenges students to explore the nature of their creative practice and how it can actively contribute to an urgent awareness movement (with Julia Melo and Rafaela Paulos)
