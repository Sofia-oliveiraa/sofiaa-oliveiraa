With the competitive increase in the television sector in recent decades, television visual identity has become an important strategic tool. A television channel is not a static and unchanging organism. On the contrary, its essence is mutable and constantly presents different aspects in an alternative manner. It should be flexible, fluid, and responsive to sociological and aesthetic changes in the audiovisual universe. Visual identity is a way of visually expressing the channel's identity, differentiating and distinguishing it. I was asked, in an academic context, to produce a refresh of the TV channel's Graphic Package.
