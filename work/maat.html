I was invited, in an academic context, to design the graphic identity for a new cultural program of the Museum of Art, Architecture, and Technology (MAAT) in Lisbon. The new program, called MAAT TRANSVERSAL, is a monthly program (fictional) to be launched in March 2020 and under the supervision of MAAT. The program runs parallel to the permanent exhibitions and aims to enliven the museum and the Belém area, complementing the long-term exhibitions with other ephemeral events in the fields of visual arts, performance, music, installation, video, and mixed media.
